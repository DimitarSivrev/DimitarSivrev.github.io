About the event

Following the success of our last tournament, the English Riviera Backgammon Congress returns from 20-22 February 2026 at The Livermead House hotel. This year we're expanding to 64 Masters and 32 Intermediates - an increase on last year with the new 32-player Intermediates draw. The tournament will be held at The Livermead House hotel, a lovely place by the coast. Join us and reserve your place early.

You can follow us for updates on our Facebook page.
