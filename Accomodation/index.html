Special Offer for Participants


Livermead House Hotel is offering an exclusive deal for the players and their companions:


£75.00 per person per night for Dinner, Bed & Breakfast on Friday and Saturday.

£70.00 per person per night for guests staying the Sunday night.

Bed & Breakfast only at the rate of £65.00 per person per night and £60.00 per person per night for the Sunday night.


The offer includes a standard English breakfast and a 4-course dinner. Prices are for a person in a single room or 2 in a double room. One person in a double room will be an additional £30.


To take advantage of the offer, contact the hotel directly and mention that you are attending the backgammon tournament.

Phone: (01803) 294361 / 294363 or Email: info@livermead.com


The English Riviera Backgammon Congress will be hosted at The Livermead House hotel in Torquay.
