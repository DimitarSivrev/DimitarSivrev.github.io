Supporting The Stroke Association

For 2025, we are excited to support The Stroke Association as our charity of choice!

They are a national charity actively working in our area, but they currently have no local fundraising efforts—so we've decided to change that.

We've set an initial £500 target for our online campaign, but we believe we can go far beyond that with your help!

Anyone who wishes to support The Stroke Association can donate via the donate button:
