Exclusive Accommodation Offer for Participants and Their Companions from Livermead House Hotel

Our kind hosts at The Livermead House Hotel have kept rates the same as the last tournament — which helps keep the weekend accessible; we'd love to welcome you in 2026.

Rates for Dinner, Bed & Breakfast:

  • Friday & Saturday: £75.00 per person per night
  • Sunday: £70.00 per person per night

Rates for Bed & Breakfast only:

  • Friday & Saturday: £65.00 per person per night
  • Sunday: £60.00 per person per night

Offer includes:

  • Standard English breakfast
  • 4-course dinner (only for Dinner, Bed & Breakfast option)
