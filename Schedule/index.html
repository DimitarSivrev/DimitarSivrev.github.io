Tournament Schedule

The schedule for the backgammon tournament is outlined below. The organizers reserve the right to make changes at any time without notice.

Throughout the tournament: There will be 8-player DMP, Speedgammon, Switchgammon, and Tapa events, subject to demand.

Day 1

(Friday - February 21st)

  • 1:00 PM: Registration opens for all events
  • 2:00 PM: Freeroll (5 pts, 8 players) starts
  • 4:00 PM: £10 Jackpot (5 pts, 16 players) starts
  • 6:00 PM: £100 Super Jackpot (9 pts, 16 players) starts
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner, bar available
  • 11:00 PM: Play finishes for the day

Day 2

(Saturday - February 22nd)

  • Buffet and bar throughout the day
  • 9:00 AM: Registration opens
  • 9:30 AM: Announcements
  • 9:45 AM: Saturday qualifying matches begin
  • 3:00 PM: Torquay Cup 1st round begins
  • 6:00 PM: 1-point tie-breaks start
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner
  • 8:00 PM: Consultation Doubles (5 pts, 8 teams) starts
  • 11:00 PM: Play finishes for the day

Day 3

(Sunday - February 23rd)

  • 9:00 AM: Registration opens
  • 9:45 AM: Play resumes - Riviera Cup elimination stage, Torquay Cup 2nd round
  • 11:00 AM: Livermead Cup starts
  • 12:30 PM: Board Tournament (3 pts, free for main players, £10 to re-enter) starts
  • 12:30 PM: Friday and Saturday events continue as necessary
  • 7:00 PM (approx.): Finish
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner for those staying another night
Date & Time

20th to 22nd February 2026

Venue

The Livermead House,
Torquay
